Jednostavna aplikacija koja prati vaše kretanje i iscrtava ga na karti. Preporučujemo da prethodno prebacite karte na telefon ili SD karticu da ne bi plaćali prenos podataka.

TrekMe takođe prikazuje pređene kilometre, trenutnu i prosječnu brzinu, GPS podatke, kalorije (približno samo za kretanje po ravnom), vrijeme kretanja, vrijeme pauziranja, promjenu nadmorske visine.

TrekMe is a simple navigation tracking app.
It can be used while running, hiking, cycling or driving.

TrekMe uses maps stored on your phone (to avoid data charging save maps on your phone or SD card) to draw a line showing your movements.
It also shows current and average speed, running time and pause time (if you pause your run), GPS data, distance.
Large live tile shows this data on home screen, medium and small tile show distance only.

This is open source app and source code is available.
https://github.com/zbabac/TrekMe.git
